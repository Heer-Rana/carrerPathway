Leadership in Management Roadmap

Foundations of Leadership

Understand the core principles and theories of leadership in management.

Key Topics:

  • Leadership Styles and Theories
  • Emotional Intelligence in Leadership
  • Ethics and Values-Based Leadership
  • Self-Awareness and Personal Development
  • Vision and Goal Setting

This module lays the groundwork for effective leadership, focusing on understanding various leadership styles and developing your personal leadership philosophy.

Reading Content: "On Becoming a Leader" by Warren Bennis.

Team Leadership and Motivation

Learn strategies for building and leading high-performing teams.

Key Topics:

  • Team Dynamics and Development
  • Motivation Theories and Practices
  • Conflict Resolution and Negotiation
  • Delegation and Empowerment
  • Building Trust and Psychological Safety

Discover how to create a positive team culture, motivate your team members, and handle conflicts effectively to drive team performance.

Reading Content: "The Five Dysfunctions of a Team" by Patrick Lencioni.

Strategic Leadership

Develop skills in strategic thinking and decision-making.

Key Topics:

  • Strategic Planning and Execution
  • Change Management
  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Risk Management and Decision Making
  • Organizational Culture and Alignment

Learn how to think strategically, lead organizational change, and foster innovation to drive your organization forward.

Reading Content: "Good to Great" by Jim Collins.

Communication and Influence

Master the art of effective communication and influencing skills.

Key Topics:

  • Interpersonal Communication Skills
  • Public Speaking and Presentation
  • Influencing and Persuasion Techniques
  • Active Listening and Feedback
  • Cross-Cultural Communication

Enhance your ability to communicate effectively, influence stakeholders, and build strong relationships across your organization.

Reading Content: "Crucial Conversations: Tools for Talking When Stakes Are High" by Kerry Patterson et al.

Leadership in the Digital Age

Explore leadership challenges and opportunities in the digital era.

Key Topics:

  • Digital Transformation and Leadership
  • Leading Remote and Virtual Teams
  • Data-Driven Decision Making
  • Agile Leadership Principles
  • Ethical Considerations in the Digital Age

Prepare yourself to lead effectively in the digital age, understanding the impact of technology on leadership and organizational success.

Reading Content: "The Digital Leader's Handbook" by Ade McCormack.
