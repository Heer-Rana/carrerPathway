Types of Architectural Drawings

Exploring architectural drawing types.

  • Floor Plans->A top view of a building layout, showing rooms, walls, doors, and windows.
  • Elevations->Exterior flat 2D views of a building from different sides.
  • Site Plans->Aerial view of a building within its surroundings.
  • Perspective Drawings->3D-like visual representation of buildings.

Reference Book: ""Architectural Graphics” by Francis D.K. Ching

Drawing Techniques

Represent 3D space on a 2D surface.

  • 1-Point Perspective->Used for interiors, roads, and long hallways.
  • 2-Point Perspective-> Common for buildings, exteriors, urban scenes.
  • 3-Point Perspective->Used for high-rise views
  • Isometric & Axonometric Views->Technical 3D-like drawings used in construction.

Reference Book: "Architecture: Form, Space, & Order" by Francis D.K. Ching
